**Key Considerations & Tips for Your CV:**

1.  **Customize for Each Application:** While this is a strong base, always tweak the summary and bullet points to align with the specific job description you're applying for. Use keywords from the job posting.
2.  **Quantify Achievements:** For future roles or as you gain more experience, try to add numbers or metrics to your bullet points (e.g., "Reduced page load time by 15%", "Handled 100+ user tickets monthly"). Even for an intern, if you contributed to a specific feature that launched, you could mention it.
3.  **GitHub Profile:** Make sure your GitHub profile is polished. Include a few personal projects that showcase your skills, especially using the technologies listed. Clean code, good READMEs, and commit history are important.
4.  **LinkedIn:** Ensure your LinkedIn profile mirrors your CV and expands on your experiences. Connect with people in your field.
5.  **Soft Skills:** While not explicitly listed, be prepared to discuss soft skills like teamwork, problem-solving, communication, and adaptability during interviews.
6.  **Proofread:** Double-check for any typos or grammatical errors.
7.  **Format:** Save your CV as a PDF to preserve formatting.
